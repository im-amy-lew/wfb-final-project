Sacramento Weavers' and Spinners' Guild

Weaving by SWSG member Jan DeShera

The Sacramento Weavers' and Spinners' Guild is a nonprofit organization that encourages growth in the art of weaving, spinning, and dyeing via the interchange of ideas, research, and contact with experts in the field. The Guild presents members with the opportunity to meet and share through meetings, workshops, and lectures.

Guild Meetings are Currently meeting via Zoom to accord with Covid protocols.

They are usually held at the Shepard Garden and Art Center, 3330 McKinley Blvd, Sacramento, CA, 10:00 a.m. on the fourth Tuesday of each month, September through May, except December.

The meetings begin with Guild business, then proceed to an educational program. Bring your lunch; coffee and cookies are provided. After lunch, we return to the presentation by the featured speaker, which may include a hands-on portion. Membership is $35 a year payable in May.

Guests are welcome. A $3 guest fee is requested.

Parking is free. Wheelchair accessible.
